Week4 - Hello

This is the first experiment for AngularJS.
Elements in the sentence below come from three places:
"Hello" from ng-controller, "" is binded with the input field, and "!" is written in html.

{{hello}} {{Name}}!

In html: 
<label for="name">Name:</label>
<input id="name" ng-model="Name" placeholder="Enter a name here">
<h1>{{hello}} {{Name}}!</h1>
In javascript: 
$scope.hello = "Hello";
