Week3 - Local Copy

In this experiment a local copy of jQuery is used, instead of linking to jQuery. Having a local copy of jQuery is good for development and debugging purpose. On the other side, linking to a minified version improves performance and is good for production purpose.

The "Hello jQuery" above is produced by script to prove jQuery is working.

$(function () {
    $("p:first").html("<b>Hello jQuery</b>");
})
